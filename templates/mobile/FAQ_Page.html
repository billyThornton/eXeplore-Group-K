Frequenty Asked Questions

Q: Why can't I see the questions at each location?

A: Only the Team Leader of your group can view questions and submit answers at each location.

.

Q: What if my group can't find a location?

A: Use the tools in the side bar and student resources to help you, the campus map on the university webite is a useful tool.

.

Q: What if I don't know my tutor?

A: To find out your tutor you can ask a member of staff in the starting room (Harrison 101) or simply join another team!

.

Q: How do we know who our Team Leader is?

A: At the beginning of your game one of your team members will have been told they are the Team Leader

.
