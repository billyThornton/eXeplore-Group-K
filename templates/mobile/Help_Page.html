Help Page

How to play:

Once you've logged in and chosen a team, you will be presented with a clue to where you need to go next.

Once you think you've reached the next location, tap 'Here' and your Team Leader will be shown a question!

Your Team Leader will need to select an answer so you can move on to the next location!

Once the progress bar at the top is full you've finished!!!

You will then be shown your teams score and leaderboard ranking!

Still unsure? Try the FAQ page!
