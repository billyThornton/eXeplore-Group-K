How to play:

Once you've chosen a type of route(Not yet implemented) you will be presented with a clue to where you need to go next.

Once you think you've reached the next location, tap 'Next' and you will be displayed with your task!

Enter your answer and move on to the next location! Once the progress bar at the top is full you've finished!!!

You will be given a score and your leaderboard position.
