Game Keeper FAQs

Q: What do I do if I get lost/get separated from my team?

A: Use the map and game progress to find where you next location should be. Also, don’t be afraid to ask others around you for guidance.

.

Q: How long should the game take for a team to complete?

A: The game should take roughly half an hour to complete, at a good pace.

.

Q: What is the purpose of this game?

A: The purpose of the game is to get freshers familiar with the university campus and locations that they will most likely frequent over their course.
